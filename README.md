## Project Overview

In this project, I developed an interactive **Tableau dashboard** to analyze sales performance, customer behavior, and operational efficiency across multiple distribution centers in the United States.

The dataset contains information about:

- Sales amounts
- Order dates
- Delivery time
- Distribution center locations
- Customer type (new vs returning)
- Customer satisfaction ratings

Using Tableau, the raw dataset was transformed into visual insights that help explain patterns in **sales trends, customer satisfaction, and logistics performance**.

---

## Tools Used

**Tableau**  
Data visualization, dashboard design, and visual analytics

**Microsoft Excel**

## Key Business Insights

From the dashboard analysis, several insights were identified:

- Some distribution centers contribute significantly higher sales than others.
- Sales trends vary monthly, indicating seasonal patterns.
- Returning customers represent a major portion of total sales.
- Customer satisfaction ratings are generally high.
- Faster delivery times contribute to higher customer satisfaction.

## Conclusion

This dashboard provides a comprehensive view of sales performance, customer behavior, and logistics efficiency.

These insights can support businesses in improving **distribution strategies, customer retention, and operational performance**.
